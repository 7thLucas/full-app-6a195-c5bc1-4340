# FruitLens — Product Information

## Overview
FruitLens is a personal fruit detection app where the user uploads a photo and the app identifies the fruit name and assesses its freshness/quality level.

## Users
- Individual consumers who want to quickly identify fruits and check their freshness before eating or purchasing.
- Home cooks and health-conscious individuals looking to ensure quality of their produce.

## Brand
- Name: FruitLens
- Personality: Fresh, clean, approachable, and informative
- Tone: Friendly, helpful, and straightforward — like a knowledgeable friend who knows their fruits

## Core Features
1. Photo Upload — Users upload a photo of a fruit from their device
2. Fruit Identification — AI-powered recognition identifies the fruit by name
3. Freshness Assessment — The app evaluates and displays the quality/freshness level of the fruit
4. Results Display — Clear, visual presentation of fruit name and freshness score/status

## Anti-References
- Avoid overly clinical or technical language
- Avoid cluttered, data-heavy dashboards
- Avoid dark, heavy design aesthetics

## Strategic Principles
- Simplicity first: The user flow should be a single, obvious action (upload a photo)
- Clarity: Results should be immediately understandable without explanation
- Delight: Small moments of visual feedback make the experience feel alive and responsive
