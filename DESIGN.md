# FruitLens — Design Guidelines

## Color Palette
- Primary: Fresh green (#4CAF50) — conveys freshness, nature, and health
- Secondary: Warm orange (#FF9800) — evokes fruit, energy, and vitality
- Accent: Soft yellow (#FFF9C4) — light, airy highlight color
- Background: Off-white (#FAFAFA) — clean, minimal base
- Surface: White (#FFFFFF) — card and panel backgrounds
- Text Primary: Dark charcoal (#212121)
- Text Secondary: Medium gray (#757575)
- Success: Green (#66BB6A)
- Warning: Amber (#FFA726)
- Error: Soft red (#EF5350)

## Typography
- Font Family: Inter (sans-serif) — clean and modern
- Heading Scale: Bold weights (700), large and prominent
- Body: Regular (400), comfortable line-height (1.6)
- Labels & Captions: Medium (500), slightly smaller

## Elevation & Shadows
- Cards: Subtle shadow (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08))
- Modals/Overlays: Medium shadow (0 8px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.12))
- Floating elements: Soft lift shadow

## Components
- Buttons: Rounded corners (rounded-full or rounded-xl), bold label, clear call-to-action
- Upload Zone: Large, centered dashed-border dropzone with icon and label
- Result Card: Clean card layout showing fruit illustration/icon, name in large text, freshness badge
- Freshness Badge: Color-coded pill — green for fresh, yellow for moderate, red for poor
- Progress/Loading: Smooth animated spinner or pulsing skeleton
- Navigation: Minimal top nav or no nav — single-screen focused experience

## Design Principles
- Whitespace: Generous padding and spacing throughout
- Imagery: Bright, natural fruit photography as accents where appropriate
- Iconography: Simple, outline-style icons
- Responsiveness: Mobile-first, works seamlessly on phones and tablets
- Accessibility: High contrast text, clear tap targets, descriptive alt text
